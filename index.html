About Me

Hi, I’m Nate Fineman. I was recruited to Princeton's men's heavyweight rowing team after years of dedication to the sport. I rowed at Oakland Strokes from 8th grade through my senior year, honing my skills and learning what it takes to succeed in collegiate rowing recruitment.

Now, I’m passionate about guiding other rowers through the recruitment process—from refining on-water technique to crafting effective communications with college coaches. My mission is to share the strategies and insights that propelled me to a top-tier program, empowering aspiring rowers to realize their dreams.
