About Me

Hi, I’m Nate Fineman. I was recruited to Princeton's men's heavyweight rowing team for the Class of 2028 after years of dedication to the sport. I rowed at Oakland Strokes from 8th grade all the way through senior year, where I honed my skills and learned what it takes to succeed in collegiate rowing recruitment.

Now, I’m passionate about helping other rowers navigate the recruitment process—from improving erg times and on-water technique to crafting effective communications with college coaches. My goal is to share the strategies and insights that helped me reach a top-tier program and empower aspiring rowers to achieve their college dreams.
